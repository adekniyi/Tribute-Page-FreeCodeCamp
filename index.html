Short history about Fela Kuti

  • 1938 - Fela Kuti was born in the year in Abeokuta, Nigeria to Olufela Ransome-Kuti
  • 1958 - Fela was sent to London to study Medicine
  • He later study Music at the Trinity College of Music, Trumphet being is preferred musical instrument
  • He formed a band called the Koola Lobitos, playing a fusion of Jazz and Highlife
  • 1960 - He married his first wife Remilekun Taylor, He had 27 in total during his days
  • 1963 - He moved back to newly independent Nigeria, spending a total of 5 years in London
  • He trained as a radio producer for the Nigerian Broadcasting Corporation and also played for some time with Victor Olaiya and his All Stars.
  • 1967 - Fela formed his own music genre called Afrobeat which is a combination of highlife, funk, jazz, salsa, calypso and traditional Nigerian Yoruba music
  • 1969 - Fela renamed his band Nigeria '70, Which he later renamed Africa'70
  • Fela set up a studio and called it Afro-Spot but was later changed to Afrika-Shrine
  • 1977 - Fela released an hit called Zombie(metaphor to describe the methods of the Nigerian military) which made Nigeria government angry
  • 1979 - He formed his own political party with the aim of becoming Nigeria President
  • 1984 - He was arrested and imprisoned by the Niegria Head of State Muhammadu Buhari
  • 1997 - Fela died at age 58
